Humidity

Humidity is recorded near 100% across Latitudes. While not specified in the API, it is clear from the values approaching 100 that this is a measurement of relative (not absolute) humidity. If it were absolute humidity, we would likely see the highest values near the equator. Not that some of the lowest values for Humidity are just north of the equator, and are likely the same cities with the highest temperature (which would confirm the hypothesis that these cities are in Northern Africa). Again, these could be easily confirmed through additional exploration.
